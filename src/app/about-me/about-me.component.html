About me Hello, my name is Samuel Haas. I am in my mid-twenties and have lived in Germany all my life. During my school years I had the opportunity to get to know the world of technology through Jugend Forscht. This small research project turned out to be so successful that I subsequently made electronics and mechanics the subject of my apprenticeship at a leading medium-sized company in the region. After more than three years in the midst of complex industrial systems with various controls and programming, my programming skills were not enough - I wanted more. Initially, I worked on my own with various books, but then I decided to take a professional further education course to become a frontend developer. It was an exciting, educational time - now that I have gained my first experience as a Fontend developer, I would like to expand on it and am looking forward to exciting and challenging projects.
